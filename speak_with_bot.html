How to work with bot

Q:
What are your working on?
A:
online at 13:00 or 42-test-yourproject#1 at 15:00
D:
This question means that you are online now. Your answer must be *online at 13:00* if you are going start later at that time or *42-test-yourproject#1 at 15:00* if you start work with ticket #1 and you`re going to end it at 15:00
Q:
Work aloud with...
A:
You should add comment to current ticket
D:
Update your progress with ticket, write about your problems too
Other commands:
A:
eta done
D:
mark current expectation done, means that you finished work before time or need pause. Next step is to set expectation about time you're going to be online.
A:
eta +10m
D:
means that you need 10 minutes more to work on current task (use also different quantity or 1h to add 1 hour).
